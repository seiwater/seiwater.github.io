Stockholm Environment Institute, Water Group

The Water Group at the Stockholm Environment Institute-US center advances integrated approaches to freshwater assessment and policy, in five main areas: methods for integrated water analysis, capacity building, modeling climate change impacts on water resources, river basin assessments, and global water futures. The team works throughout the US, Central and South America and the Caribbean, the Middle East, Africa and Asia.
